About Guruvayoor

Guruvayoor is one of the most sacred and revered temples in Kerala, dedicated to Lord Krishna, an incarnation of Lord Vishnu. Known as the 'Dwarka of the South', thousands of devotees visit every day seeking blessings and spiritual peace.
