Dress Code for Guruvayoor Temple

The Guruvayoor Temple follows a strict traditional dress code to maintain the sanctity of the shrine. Visitors are expected to adhere to these guidelines before entering the temple premises.

For Men

  • Must wear a mundu/dhoti tied around the waist without a shirt
  • Vests, lungis, jeans, and trousers are not permitted inside the temple
  • Upper body must remain bare; a traditional angavastram can be draped over the shoulder

For Women

  • Saree, set-mundu, salwar kameez, or long skirt with blouse is allowed
  • Short skirts, jeans, leggings, and western wear are not permitted
  • Modest dressing is expected, covering shoulders and knees

For Children

  • Boys may wear shorts, dhotis, or mundus
  • Girls may wear pavada, long skirt, or salwar kameez
Important Note

Photography, mobile phones, and modern attire are strictly prohibited inside the temple.
